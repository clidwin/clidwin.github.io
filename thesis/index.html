Abstract

Why do people have a fear of data, especially if the information is about themselves? Often times, we fear what we cannot understand, and the concept of raw data scares many people because they fear how it will be used. But what if there was a way for people to understand what their data is and how it is used? How might we go about educating people about the quantified self and provide inviting ways to interact with their data? This thesis explores both the process for creating and the design of an ambient display that presents personal data in an interactive and visual manner.
